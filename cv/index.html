Professional Summary

Cryptographer working on the foundations and real-world deployment of advanced public-key encryption. I design provably secure functional, attribute-based, and registered encryption schemes that decentralize trust, make computation on encrypted data verifiable, and bring strong privacy guarantees to cloud platforms, digital infrastructure, and AI systems. Author of 20+ peer-reviewed papers at leading venues including Crypto, Eurocrypt, Asiacrypt, PKC, and the Journal of Cryptology.
