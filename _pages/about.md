I am a researcher of the KASTEL Security Research Labs (Secure Computation &amp; Communication group) within the
[Research Group Rupp](https://crypto.iti.kit.edu/english/research_group_rupp.php),
headed by [Andy Rupp](https://crypto.kastel.kit.edu/english/head_of_group.php), under [KASTEL: Cryptography and Security Group](https://crypto.kastel.kit.edu/english/index.php),
at the [Karlsruhe Institute of Technology](https://www.kit.edu/english/).

Previously, I was a postdoc at the [Social Informatics Laboratories](https://www.rd.ntt/e/organization/researcher/?lab=1015)
of NTT Japan, hosted by [Ryo Nishimaki](https://www.nishimaki.info). I obtained my PhD from the Department of
[Mathematics](http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/department/MA), [IIT Kharagpur](http://www.iitkgp.ac.in), under the supervision
of [Prof. Ratna Dutta](http://www.facweb.iitkgp.ac.in/~ratna/). I have also been a research intern at the
[CIS Laboratories](https://ntt-research.com/cis/) of NTT Research, working with
[Pratish Datta](https://ntt-research.com/cis-people/) on computing over encrypted data, and a visiting scientist at the
[R. C. Bose Centre for Cryptology and Security](https://www.isical.ac.in/~rcbose/), ISI Kolkata, hosted by
[Prof. Mridul Nandi](https://www.isical.ac.in/~mridul/).

My research designs provably-secure cryptographic primitives for computing on encrypted data &mdash; spanning
**functional encryption** (attribute-based, registered, and multi-authority variants), **anamorphic cryptography**,
**verifiable computation**, and **privacy-enhancing technologies** &mdash; with an eye toward decentralized trust and
privacy-preserving digital infrastructure.
